## CHECKLIST OF RECOMMENDED PHYSICAL DOCUMENTS

| # | Document | Purpose | Who Signs |
|---|----------|---------|-----------|
| 1 | **Letter Request to Conduct Study** | Formal permission to deploy in partner barangay | Researchers + Adviser + Punong Barangay |
| 2 | **Barangay Endorsement & Permit** | Proves LGU/Barangay authorized the study | Punong Barangay + Barangay Secretary |
| 3 | **Certificate of Deployment & Acceptance** | Proves the system was actually deployed and used | Punong Barangay + Barangay Secretary |
| 4 | **Informed Consent Forms** | Ethics compliance — respondents agreed to participate | Each survey respondent |
| 5 | **Certificate of Appearance / Conduct** *(optional)* | Proof researchers physically went to the barangay | Punong Barangay |
| 6 | **Thesis Adviser Endorsement** *(if required by AdNU)* | Academic approval to conduct the study | Thesis Adviser |

### NOTES:
- **Documents 1–4 are the most critical.** Panelists typically look for proof of LGU consent, deployment evidence, and ethical compliance.
- Print on **short bond paper** (8.5" x 11") unless your department specifies otherwise.
- If possible, have the barangay documents **stamped with the official barangay seal** in addition to the signature.
- Keep **originals** and include **photocopies in your thesis appendix**.
- Document 3 (Informed Consent) should be given to **every survey respondent** — both pre-test and post-evaluation.
